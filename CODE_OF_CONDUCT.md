# Code of Conduct

Be direct, respectful, and public-data safe.

## Expected behavior

- Keep discussion technical and constructive.
- Respect privacy boundaries around vault content.
- Use synthetic examples when demonstrating bugs.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Posting private notes, credentials, or sensitive data.
- Encouraging exfiltration or unsafe automation against personal knowledge bases.

Maintainers may remove content or block participation that violates these expectations.
